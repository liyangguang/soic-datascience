Tuition and Fees

Indiana University is committed to keeping its degrees affordable for in-state and out-of-state students, both through low costs and generous financial aid.

The Office of Student Financial Assistance has information about current costs of attendance for full-time, domestic graduate students.

IU's Office of the Bursar maintians current rates for credits hours, mandatory fees, miscellaneous fees, and more. They also have a Tuition Estimator where you can select your term, residency, and program.

The Office of International Services has costs for international students and include mandatory health insurance.
